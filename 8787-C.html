Team 8787-C

8787-C is Eastwood's first elite all-girl VEX team that was founded in the 2015-2016 season. That same year at the West Texas State Championship, they won both the Excellence and Tournament Champions Awards that qualified them to VEX World Comptition. There, the girls were blessed to be the first Eastwood Robotics team to win the Teamwork Award.
