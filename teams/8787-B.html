Team 8787-B

Team 8787-B is a group of good friends that understand each other and work towards making the most efficient robot. Team 8787-B has been a very strong team in west texas and will strive to keep that a goal for our team. We also strive to help the whole robotics community in west texas buy helping smaller teams improve themselves.
