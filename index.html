Who We Are

Eastwood Robotics, or team 8787, is an elite robotics team based in El Paso, Texas. We have gone to the Vex Robotics World Competition three Times and have come back home with multiple awards such as the Teamwork Award and the Volunteer of the Year Award. Eastwood Robotics continues to strive for excellence in the 2017-2018 year by extending its knowledge to the El Paso community by doing things such as hosting the largest girl powered event in the world and hosting multiple robotics workshops throughout the year.
